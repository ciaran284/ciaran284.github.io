Bio

Nerub carefully lures his enemies in, using his webs to keep them stuck on the ground, as his scrabs and locusts begin to tear apart at thier flesh. When his webs aren't enough, his ultimate ability terrifies his opponents, forcing them to run away from him, even if it means running towards doom.
