Battlefield Medicine

Increases an ally's health regeneration and amplifies all healing received by the target. If the target dies during the buff, their respawn time is lowered. Applies a dispel on both the caster and the target on cast.


Self Aid

When the Apostate takes damage, after a delay he heals for a percentage of the damage taken.


Blasphemy

Whenever the Apostate heals for more than the stack threshold, relative to his maximum health, he gains a Blasphemy Charge. On activation, releases all charges to deal damage to nearby enemies and heal nearby allies. Blasphemy Charges are lost on death.



Bio

In denial of faith, the Apostate heals his allies with his own repurposed ideals. Able to defy his opponents and their aggression with his self aid, his blasphemy can turn the pain of battle into power to crush anyone who stands in his way.
