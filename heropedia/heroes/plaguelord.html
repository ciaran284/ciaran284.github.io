Life Drain

Plague Lord's attacks place and refresh a stacking debuff on enemies, then restore health and mana to him based on damage dealt and the amount of stacks, while slowing the target.


Toxic Form

Become an immaterial cloud of toxicity, poisoning each enemy you come into contact with. While in the cloud, Plague Lord is undetectable and disarmed, while gaining bonus movement speed.


Stalking Stepsis

Places a sepsis on the target location. Can be activated again to detonate it, dealing damage to all enemies in an area. The damage increases as the sepsis stays dormant. Only one sepsis can exist at a time.


Rapid Growth

The Plague Lord grows with each instance of damage dealt by him, temporarily increasing his attack speed and mana regeneration.



Bio

Wandering the dark trenches of war and death, Elefis scours the land with his ever growing pestilence. With his incredibly Rapid Growth, he quickly overpowers and overwhelms his opponents and brings their destruction in the form of a terrifying and virulent end.
