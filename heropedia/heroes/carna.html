Spit Blood

Spits blood in a line. Enemies affected take damage each second, are slowed, and have a chance to miss their attacks.


Spellsoak

At the cost of some health, puts a barrier on an allied hero. The barrier absorbs magical damage. Applies a dispel on the target.


Hook Impale

Impales an enemy on Carna's hook, stunning them and dealing physical damage relative to Carna's maximum health.


Consume

Carna attempts to eat target creep. If the creep is below a health threshold, it's consumed and Carna gains a permanent stack of Well Fed. If the creep is above the threshold, it takes damage. Each stack of Well Fed increases Carna's Health and Health Regeneration.


Hemorrhage

Carna starts to emit blood and gasses around him, continously dealing damage relative to his maximum health to nearby enemies. Can be cast while stunned.



Bio

The ravenous Carna empowers his life pool by gorging on the flesh of the enemy creeps. He sprays cursed blood on his enemies that damages them and impairs thier chance to land attacks, preparing them for his feast, using his gained lifeforce agressively to disable and critically damage his opponents based on the difference in their health.
