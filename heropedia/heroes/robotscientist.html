Emergency Minefield

The Robot places a randomly generated minefield around it. The mines explode for physical damage when an enemy steps on them. Deals damage to buildings.


Cross-Hook

The Robot shoots four hooks in cardinal directions. A hook grabs the first enemy hit, rooting them. After the root, each target is pulled to the center of the spell.


Goblin Bomb

Throws a bomb towards the target point. The bomb explodes after a delay, damaging all enemies in the area. If the bomb lands on an enemy, it follows them for the duration. Attaching to an enemy without a bomb reduces cooldown. Deals damage to buildings.



Full Missile Jacket

The Robot channels for up to 10 seconds, constantly sending rockets towards the closest enemy hero.



Bio

The Robot Scientist equips himself with many of the quirks of goblin technology, using bombs and mines to punish the unwary and destroy everything in his sight. Anyone who is not prepared for his missile barrage will find themselves wondering when it ends all the way to thier grave.
