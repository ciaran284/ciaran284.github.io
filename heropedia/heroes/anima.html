Stellar Barrage

Vector targeted. Destructive meteor waves fall at an interval four times along a line, dealing damage to units hit. Their areas do not overlap. A wave will deal extra damage if the target was already hit by a previous wave this cast.


Search and Obliterate

Each second, 2 projectiles are shot at the nearest enemy units. A projectile deals damage on hit, and a lower amount over time.


Field of Despair

Creates an extremely large area which withers the life of enemies inside, dealing damage relative to their max health, and slowing their movement.



Resequencing

Applies a slow and silence to enemies in the area. After this debuff expires, the enemies are stunned.



Bio

Anima, the World Ender, Bane of Stars, Obliterates his foes with no mercy. Capable of crushing enemies from any angle with his Stellar Barrage, he proves that there is no Celestial Body that can't be destroyed, with enough power..
