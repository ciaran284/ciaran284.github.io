Starfall

Channels for up to 5 seconds. Each second, stars fall from the sky onto nearby enemies. Units hit by the stars are increasingly slowed and have their magic resistance reduced, and take damage.


Vindictive Strike

Proxima Centauri charges its star energy, becoming attack immune for a short duration. Afterwards, a projectile is shot towards the nearest enemy hero, which damages and disarms.


Moment of Respite

Proxima Centauri briefly puts an allied hero to sleep between the stars. Hides the target and makes it invulnerable. After the duration, enemies around the target are stunned.



Celestial Link

Connects with target enemy hero, leashing it to Proxima Centauri. Each second, a percentage of the target's current health and mana is drained by the caster. If the linked heroes go far enough from each other, the leash breaks, slowing the target.



Bio

Proxima Centauri is an enigmatic being that uses the potentially infinite light energy within it to cleanse the battlefield of its enemies. Many other stars will fall in its wake, but Proxima Centauri is the first, and will not rest until everything is bathed in its light.
