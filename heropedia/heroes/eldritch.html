Spawn Abomination

The Eldritch throws a chunk of itself to the target point. Upon hitting the ground, magical damage is dealt around it, and an Abomination spawns.

Passively, makes the Eldritch leave behind an Abomination on death.



Non-Euclidean Design

Forces an impossible idea into the target hero's mind, stunning them and burning their mana.


Corrupting Field

Corrupts target area, causing enemies standing in it to take magical damage every second. Allies standing in the area are healed instead, and gain increased attack speed.



Become Abomination

After a delay, replaces a target abomination anywhere on the map with the Eldritch.


End of All That Is

After a delay, targetted area is struck by meteors, stunning and burning all enemies in it. Global cast range.



Bio

Coming from an unknown dimension, Eldritch spreads his influence like a plague, sending abominations across the map to find and harass his foes, before beckoning down the apocalypse to bring his enemies to an untimely end.
