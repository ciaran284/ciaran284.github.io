Bio

The embodiment of cold, Nahl uses his varied arsenal of abilities to activate his precarious icicle that hangs overhead, silencing and crushing enemies. Every spell he casts creates a wave of ice that blasts from him, and his unending agression can lead to a swift and merciless demise for his enemies.
