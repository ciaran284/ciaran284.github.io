Fundamental Power

The White Magician gathers cosmic energy, refreshing the cooldowns of all his other spells and items. Grants a mana regeneration buff.



Bio

The versatile and ever learning White Magician never struggles to outmaneuver his opponents. Equipped with an arsenal of destructive spells that can manipulate and cripple his enemies, his true power lowers in his ability to strike twice. A good magician never truly runs out of tricks.
