Lucifer tries sending target enemy hero to hell, dealing pure damage relative to the target's maximum health, while healing himself for a percentage of his own health. Bio

The brutal and unstoppable Demon King, Lucifer, overwhelms his foes with his burning presence and endurance. Inflicting the Number of the Beast onto his foes, and running them down, few can hope to escape his grasp.
