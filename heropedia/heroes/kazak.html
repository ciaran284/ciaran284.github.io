Bio

The rabid ursine fighter Kazak never allows his prey to escape, punishing fleeing foes with painfully damaging backstabs. In the jungle he is able to quickly clear through the neutral creatures, acquiring the resources he needs to get on top of his opponents and make them run for their deaths.
