Soul-Tearing Thrust

Ghostblade

Ghost Form

After a fade delay, Ethereal Lancer gains movement speed, phased movement, and enters ghost form, which renders him unable to be attacked. Attacking or taking damage breaks ghost form. Additionally, provides a permanent increase of agility.



Bio

A fearsome and evasive fighter, the Ethereal Lancer walks the plane of the living and the ethereal, using the power of both realms to destroy his enemies without ever taking a scratch from their physical weapons or attacks.
