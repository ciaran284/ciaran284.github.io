Bio

Manipulative and cunning, the Kingseeker uses his King and his shadowy army to overwhelm his enemies, gaining and granting damage for every ally he has. Protecting himself and his allies with a damage absorbing glpyh, he can quickly turn the tides on his opponents and crush them with his King's power.
