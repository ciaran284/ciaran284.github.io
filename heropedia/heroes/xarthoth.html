Brutality

Xartoth's next basic attack in 3.5 seconds has increased damage, and gives him a Brutality stack. If Xartoth already has 2 Brutality stacks, the attack consumes them to grant him an attack speed buff and stun the target.


Ichor Eruption

Xartoth slows his movement speed as he charges up over 2 seconds. When fully charged, it automatically releases, dealing damage and stunning all nearby enemies. If released earlier by reactivating the spell, deals half damage and slows instead of stunning.


Blood Mark

When cast, marks the closest enemy hero on the map and reveals it for the duration. When attacking or moving towards the marked hero, Xartoth gains movement speed and attack damage. If he kills the target, he gains bonus gold.



Artery Burst

Weakens an enemy hero, causing all hero attacks against it to deal physical damage relative to the target's current health. If the target falls below a health threshold during the debuff, it is executed, and Xartoth heals for a percentage of his maximum health.



Bio

Xartoth, the disciple of an old clan of blood worshippers who use deadly blood magic to mercilessly dispatch their enemies. Xartoth will hunt his prey with an overwhelming zeal, finding the nearest victim to chase without restraint repelling anyone who tries to intervene in his quarry, who he always ensures a quick and painful death.
