Polymorph

The Trickster pranks an enemy, turning them into a random critter, preventing them from attacking, casting, or using items. While in this form, the target is slowed and takes additional damage from all sources.


Inverse Bolt

A magical bolt is shot from a fixed distance towards the Trickster. If the bolt hits an enemy on its way, it instantly ends, dealing damage, and healing the caster for a percentage of damage dealt.


Panic Attack

The Trickster turns invisible, gaining a small boost of movement speed and the ability to move through units. Attacking an enemy to break invisibility causes it to run away in panic.


Hell's Impale

After a short delay, summons a spike under the target enemy, which knocks them upwards. Upon landing, the target takes damage based on their level, and is stunned.



Bio

The Trickster manipulates his enemies expectations, deceiving their senses with his Inverse Bolt and toying with their ability to react through polymorphic hexes and fear inducing camouflage. When the Trickster is done toying with his enemies, he finishes them off with a devastating impalement from his Hell's Impale.
